Vanilla Drop Cake

  • Ingredients
  • Directions
  • Baking
  • Icing

Description: Cakes

A drop cake is mixed and dropped on a baking sheet much like cookies would be made.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla bean
  • Butter
  • Sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees
  2. Add dry ingredients
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth
  4. Bake for 1 hour
  5. Cool cakes on cooling rack for 2 hours
  6. Frost, chill, and enjoy!
